What is Hetzner Cloud?

Hetzner is a German web hosting company founded in 1997, known for offering some of the most competitively priced dedicated servers in Europe. Their cloud division, Hetzner Cloud, brings this same value proposition to VPS hosting — delivering NVMe-based virtual servers at prices that make competitors look expensive.

While Vultr and DigitalOcean dominate the US market, Hetzner has built a loyal following among European users and international projects that value German engineering quality at budget prices. Their €3.71/month CX21 plan (2 vCPU, 4GB RAM, 40GB NVMe) regularly tops "best value VPS" lists.

💡 Key insight: Hetzner's pricing is in Euros, so US customers pay ~8-10% more due to exchange rates. Even accounting for this, Hetzner often undercuts competitors on price-to-performance.

Key Features of Hetzner Cloud

🇩🇪 German Quality, German Reliability

Hetzner owns and operates its own data centers in Germany and Finland — no third-party hosting. This means tighter control over hardware quality, cooling, and network infrastructure. Their data centers run on 100% renewable energy, a major plus for environmentally conscious projects.

💾 All NVMe, All the Time

Every Hetzner Cloud plan includes NVMe storage — no premium tier needed. This is unique in the VPS market. Whether you're paying €5.78 or €100+, you get NVMe performance.

🔒 European Data Privacy

GDPR compliance is built-in. Your data stays in Germany or Finland — ideal for European businesses, agencies, or anyone concerned about US surveillance programs. No GDPR headaches.

📊 Generous Bandwidth

All plans include 20TB of outgoing bandwidth — more than enough for most projects. After 20TB, excess is billed at €1/TB. Inbound is always free.

🔧 Simple, No-Nonsense Features

Hetzner doesn't try to be everything to everyone. You get: snapshots, floating IPs, firewall, load balancers, and block storage. That's it. No managed databases, no fancy app platform, no marketplace. For power users who want control, this simplicity is a feature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hetzner Cloud good for beginners?

Hetzner Cloud is slightly less beginner-friendly than DigitalOcean. The documentation is primarily in German, and there's no one-click marketplace. However, if you're comfortable with basic Linux administration, Hetzner offers exceptional value. DigitalOcean may be easier if you're just starting out.

Hetzner vs Vultr — which is better?

For European users: Hetzner wins on price-performance with NVMe at €5.78. For global coverage: Vultr wins with 25+ data centers worldwide. For US-based projects: Vultr's High Frequency plans offer better latency. Choose based on your primary audience location.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes — Hetzner offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. This gives you a full month to test their infrastructure risk-free.

Does Hetzner offer Windows VPS?

No, Hetzner Cloud only offers Linux-based virtual servers. If you need Windows, consider Interserver which includes Windows VPS on all plans.

How does Hetzner's pricing work with USD?

Hetzner's prices are in Euros (€). At current exchange rates (~$1.08/€), the CX21 costs approximately $6.24/month. Still excellent value for the specs, but DigitalOcean's $4 Basic Droplet is technically cheaper for absolute beginners — though you get less RAM and slower storage.

Is Hetzner really 100% renewable energy?

Yes — Hetzner operates its own data centers powered entirely by renewable energy. They use a combination of hydroelectric, solar, and wind power. This is one of Hetzner's unique selling points for environmentally conscious projects.
